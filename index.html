FUN FACT

Minionese, sometimes called Banana Language because of the Minions' love of the yellow-skinned fruit, is a combination of several real languages, including French, Italian, Spanish, Indian, Japanese, and, of course, English, among others.


One example of how these words are blended together to create the jumbled language comes from Despicable Me 2, when one of the Minions utters "poulet tikka masala" into an intercom in hopes of unlocking a door. The phrase is a combination of French and Indian that describes the popular Indian-style chicken dish.
